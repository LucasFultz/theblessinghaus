The Blessing Haus

Professional Services for International Workers

The Blessing Haus is not a business, it is an idea put into practice. Also, this website is still under construction.

Mission

The mission of The Blessing Haus is simple: to make professional mental health services accessible to Missionaries and International Workers, regardless of one's ability to pay.

Vision

To provide grounded, accessible support for international workers through clarity and care.
