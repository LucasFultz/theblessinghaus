About

Lucas, his wife and their two kids live in the Black Forest of southwest Germany. Born and raised in Minnesota, Lucas graduated from Crown College with a degree in Psychology and a second degree, a double major in Intercultural Studies and Christian Studies. After college, Lucas worked in the ResLife program at Black Forest Academy for a few years where he met his wife, a teacher at the bilingual elementary school associated with BFA. After getting married, Lucas and his wife lived in Wheaton, IL for a few years while Lucas earned in MA in Marriage and Family Therapy. In 2021 they moved back to Germany and have been serving as International Workers and raising their two children.

Approach

Humans are designed with thoughts and emotions, souls and bodies, and to be in relationship with others (heart, soul, mind, strength, and neighbors). When we understand how each area of life affects and is affected by the other areas, we can then make intentional choices the allows us to be healthier as individuals and in relationship. Lucas utilizes elements of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Bowen Family Systems theory to promote health and growth in line with the client's goals. Lucas is also trained in Narrative Exposure Therapy, a research-backed, highly effective format for engaging with symptoms of mental health trauma.
